# Preset — Soft / warm / editorial-premium

Load only if the brief wants warm, generous, layered, fluid (wellness, lifestyle, premium
consumer, editorial). Overrides dials toward `VARIANCE 6 / MOTION 6 / DENSITY 3`. Foundations
and a11y (`10`–`23`) still apply.

## The look

Warmth and generosity. Soft, tinted neutrals, layered depth, rounded but disciplined shapes,
fluid motion, refined type with real editorial character. Feels crafted and calm, not corporate.

## Rules

1. **Warm, tinted palette.** Neutrals carry hue (`11`); soft, considered accents; tasteful use of
   duotone or a single restrained gradient is allowed here (unlike other presets) — but as
   texture, not as a substitute for hierarchy.
2. **Layered depth, softly.** Gentle elevation, generous radius (still from a scale, `14`),
   overlapping editorial compositions at moderate VARIANCE.
3. **Refined typography.** An editorial display face is appropriate here (the one preset where
   serif/display can lead); pair with a clean text family; generous line-height and measure (`12`).
4. **Generous space, low density.** Room to breathe; imagery-forward; one idea per view (`13`).
5. **Fluid motion** (`40`/`41`): smooth, spring-based, gentle reveals — justified and reduced-
   motion safe (`23`). This is the preset where more motion fits, but it still earns its place.
6. **Real, warm imagery.** Photography or crafted illustration with a consistent treatment — not
   filler stock, not blob SVGs (`30`, `81`).

## Avoid

Tipping into saccharine or generic "wellness template". Keep hierarchy crisp and primary actions
obvious under all the softness. Soft ≠ vague. Gradients as a crutch (only as subtle texture).
